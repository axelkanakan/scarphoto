Peirot Bistrot

April 16, 2013

After working at Pierrot Bistrot in Boston, my French is now close to perfect thanks to the chef and wait staff. As soon as you walk into this charming restaurant you are immediately transported to the South of France. Its ambiance is perfect for both business lunches and romantic dinners. How is the food you may ask? Well judging by the numbers on my scale it is délicieux!
